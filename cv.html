Zappling, A Division of 2RW


2RW is a sustainable energy consulting company that works on LEED-certification and comprehensive green energy solutions. In English: we help buildings, campuses, and governments save money and tread lightly on the planet through efficient energy usage. Zappling is 2RW’s software division, and I’m the co-head of web development there.

I write software that tracks, monitors, and analyzes a compendium of data from affordable housing properties to make detailed engineering calculations related to energy use.

I’ve also developed API architecture that connects several related web apps through a common interface to reuse building information, streamline client web services, implement internal single sign-ons, and handle user roles across all apps.

A day in the life at Zappling for me includes issue tracking via GitHub Issues, training teammates to adopt new workflows, troubleshooting IT problems across all departments, testing, scheduling DB backups, and data syncing between environments. Frequently, I can also be found implementing Heroku CI/CD for Rails apps to augment workflows and ensure new code has adequate test coverage.

Commonwealth Joe

In 2012, I co-founded a coffee roast retailer, Commonwealth Joe. In addition to the wearing-all-hats that happens in an early-stage startup, I served as the CTO. I oversaw the improvement and evolution of all technical parts of the business, including e-commerce integration with Shopify. I also envisioned and developed our entire cold brew line, which is now the fastest-growing part of CJoe.

Commonwealth Joe has been a huge success. We currently have two flagship stores, a roastery, and a brewery that provides nitro cold brew to offices up and down the east coast. To date we have raised over $2 million in venture funding. While my co-founder Robbie now runs the business full-time as CEO, I have stepped away but continue to serve in an advisory role.
